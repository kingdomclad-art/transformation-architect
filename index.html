Common Questions

How is this different from hiring a consultant for a one-time audit?

One-time audits give you a snapshot. Monthly oversight gives you a movie. Problems reveal themselves over time‚Äîstaff behavior, parent patterns, financial irregularities. Consistent monitoring catches issues early, prevents major crises, and builds accountability.

Won't my headteacher/staff resist unannounced visits?

Initially, maybe. But good professionals welcome accountability. If someone resists oversight, that's information itself. We introduce this as "quality assurance partnership" and most teams adapt within 1-2 months. Resistance usually signals something to hide.

What if you find serious problems?

I report everything directly to you with evidence, context, and recommended actions. You decide how to respond. I can provide guidance on addressing issues, or simply document for your own decision-making. Serious issues (theft, abuse, safety risks) are flagged immediately, not saved for monthly reports.

Can I start with just one school if I own multiple?

Absolutely. Many clients start with their most concerning school or their flagship location. Once you see the value, expansion is simple.

I live abroad‚Äîhow do we communicate?

Monthly reports are delivered via email/WhatsApp with photos and documentation. Quarterly calls are scheduled at your convenience (considering time zones). Urgent issues are communicated immediately via WhatsApp. You'll never wonder what's happening‚Äîyou'll know.

What if my school is far from Nairobi?

I currently serve schools within 150km of Nairobi, covering much of Central Kenya and surrounding areas. For schools further out, we can discuss customized arrangements or quarterly intensive oversight visits.

Do you sign confidentiality agreements?

Yes. Everything I observe stays between us. Your school's information, financials, and challenges are completely confidential.
